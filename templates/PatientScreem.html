Our team

A member of our staff who will assist you will complete the other information we request when it comes to treatment. Our hospital staff is comprised of only the most talented, active, and dedicated individuals in the chosen profession, committed to the hospital's vision of innovation and collaboration in community healthcare.

Our nurses

The nurses at Martin Luther King, Jr. Community Hospital are highly qualified and have specialized knowledge for the departments in which they work. Regardless of the specialty of the service line, each one is committed to prioritizing patient care and ensuring its follow-up according to scheduled care.

They are the example of the profession, each of our nurses is highly qualified to assess the patient's condition, communicate with doctors and other health professionals, and provide a thorough evaluation as required. Many of our nurses come from the surrounding community and, as with all of our staff members, from doctors to technicians and volunteers, are dedicated to serving our community.
